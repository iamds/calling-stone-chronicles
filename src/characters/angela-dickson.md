Tommy Maidment has been a friend of Gracie's since nursery where he managed to throw paint over her. They have known each other since 'forever.' Tommy is clever, very good at Maths and he supports Gracie as she studies for GCSE. He is in her Art class and often recognises how good her Art is. He is an excellent footballer, and hopes to go professional. He also hopes to try out for the county squad as a stepping stone to his dream. 

Only once does Tommy become less than a friend. The pain of that incident is hard for Gracie to handle. When texts, messages and phone fall on deaf ears she wonders if they will ever be friends again.  

He is blond, athletic and a huge support to Gracie. He protects her, cares for her and when the 'Hamlet' video, is filmed, he desperately tries to get to her. 

On more than one occasion he becomes involved in a knife attack situation. Not wanting to play the hero, Tommy most definitely is one.
