Angela Dickson is the one person Gracie wishes would leave her alone. On a daily basis, Angela, taunts her, ridicules her and extorts money, food and anything else from her. Rarely alone, Angela usually corners Gracie, with the help of Tina and Kate her two friends. 

She undermines Gracie's confidence on a regular basis. The pretty to look at and ugly natured, why Angela is the way she is we are never told. She finds ever increasing ways to make Gracie suffer and never lets up. 

Blonde, attractive and with a string of boyfriends she will find any excuse, any moment, any opportunity to make Gracie suffer.  

She is found in Book 1 'Threads of  Trial and Light'.
