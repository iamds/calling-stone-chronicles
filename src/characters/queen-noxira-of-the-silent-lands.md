Queen Noxira is the Queen of The Silent Lands. Beautiful, deadly and ruthless. She plays with people, taunts and uses them as she sees fit. Attracted to those she recognises as beautiful or handsome, she is easily bored. Her throne set in front of what appears to be a gaping mouth, is vast. 

 From Book Four - 'Weaves of Wisdom and Resolve':

At one end, dominating everything, is an enormous throne. Seated there is a pale-skinned woman. Beautiful.Ebony-black hair flows down her back, shimmering like slow tar. Her eyes, dark as coal, burn in their sockets, fixed on us. Her ruby-red mouth matches the scarlet tips of her nails. One hand caresses the shoulder of a crouching male fae at her feet. She wears an elaborate red-and-gold crown high upon her head.Her gown, which barely covers her chest, is of the finest silk. It spills around her feet like a pool of midnight.
