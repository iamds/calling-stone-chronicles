Tavian is the second son of King Edmund and Queen Serena of Limskada. He was never educated to be a king, that was always for his elder brother Dara. Tavian is full of fun and laughter. He loves stories, myths and legends and tells them like an actor! He is kindhearted, loyal and a terrible flirt. On the quest with Gracie he introduces her to 'Dragon's Kiss' a hot intoxicating drink, luckily for her there are no side effects! He is a brilliant swordsman, probably the best in Limskada. Early in book 1 he has a training session with Gracie, who, taught by Quinn, almost outsmarts him. He finds it funny, Dara finds it a problem. 

His stories fill the nights of the quest with knowledge, fun and laughter and of course, comments on each by Quinn. 

He supports Gracie, when everyone else, apart from Temerin and Quinn, see her as a threat. He stands by her, and helps her. He is a true, loyal, trustworthy friend.
