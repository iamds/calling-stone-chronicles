Temerin is the first Fae Gracie meets in Threads of Trial and Light. Quinn tells her he wields Seelie magic and is High Fae. He has long white hair, a beard and incredibly blue eyes. He is kind to Gracie from the beginning when she finds herself thrown into Limskada. He explains things to her, tells her how time works in this new place. He informs her about runes and patterns. He puts up with her mistakes, her temper and her disasters as she learns magic. He even fights her with a sword! He becomes a dear and important friend to her.

He is a friend of King Edmund and Queen Serena of Limskada, perhaps because he has helped them in the past, and tolerated by Prince Dara who distrusts anything to do with magic, especially the Fae.

He stays with her on the quest to find the missing Callisvaan and like Gracie wears one of the three. He protects her when others accuse her. Stands by her side through everything that happens to her. He guides her, supports her and sometimes, lectures her in language she rarely understands. \
He has a tendency to speak in riddles, but his trust in her is complete. 

A kind, loyal and special companion.
