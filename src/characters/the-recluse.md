The Recluse trained on Ystme. He once worked alongside Temerin. He thinks of the mage as his one time friend. But he became disillusioned by the politics and problems he saw on the magical island. He appears old, grey braids, ink-stained hands. In Book 2 he helps Gracie, Tavian and Quinn, by giving them items which will help reveal what is happening once the Envoy arrives. The items are Inkthirst, a magical stone which reveals  that which is hidden. Oathglass - a sliver of crystal which warms when a listening glamour is near, and The Whispercatcher, a crystal sphere, milky, veined with blue spirals, it catches words others wish would vanish. 

We only hear of him in Book 2, but his support is invaluable.
