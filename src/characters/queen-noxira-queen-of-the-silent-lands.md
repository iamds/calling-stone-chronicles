Queen Noxira, Queen of The Silent Lands appears only in Book Four, Weaves of Wisdom and resolve. She is stunningly beautiful, extremely dangerous and enjoys playing her deadly games with people who are stupid enough to venture into her realm, under the earth. She has no time for Gracie, taunts her as 'Gleamweaver' and finds her dull.  Her attention becomes focused on Tavian. 

From Book Four, "Weaves of Wisdom and Resolve"



At one end, dominating everything, isan enormous throne. Seated there is a pale-skinned woman. Beautiful.Ebony-black hair flows down her back, shimmering like slow tar. Her eyes, darkas coal, burn in their sockets, fixed on us. Her ruby-red mouth matches thescarlet tips of her nails. One hand caresses the shoulder of a crouching malefae at her feet. She wears an elaborate red-and-gold crown high upon her head.Her gown, which barely covers her chest, is of the finest silk. It spillsaround her feet like a pool of midnight.
