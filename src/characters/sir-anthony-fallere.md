Sir Anthony Fallere, is a judgmental,  guard with a permanent scowl. He is loyal to the princes and takes delight in making Gracie feel uncomfortable. His mouth runs away from him in Book 2. He is only interested in magic if it works to his advantage. He initially sees Gracie as a hindrance and 'child'. 

When it becomes clear, Gracie is more than able to hold her own and perhaps important, he resorts to snide, often rude comments and immature behaviour in an effort to make her look stupid. 

And in Book 2 he becomes more than an irritation. His rudeness, insulting and dangerous behaviour become a threat.
