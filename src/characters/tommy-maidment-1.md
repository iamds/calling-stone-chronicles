Tommy Maidment has known Gracie since they were at nursery together and he managed to throw paint over her. They have stayed good friends ever since. Tommy supports her, protects her and bar one incident in year eleven, is loyal, trustworthy and someone she can count on when things are tough. 

Tommy is popular. Blond, athletic and clever, he supports Gracie with maths, and takes Art with her. He is solid, dependable and a leader. An important member of the school football team, he hopes to try out for the county squad as a step closer to his football dream. 

When, at the school dance, Gracie is subjected to the filming of the 'Hamlet video', in Book 2, Strands of Shadow and Truth, Tommy rushes to find her. When they are out together, in book 1 'Threads of Trial and Light,'  he is the object of a knife attack. His mother is a teacher and his father an engineer. They are hardworking people determined to do the best for their son who they love very much.
