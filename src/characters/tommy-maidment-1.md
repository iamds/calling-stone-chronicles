Seth Ravenwood is the new man in Anne- Marie Fellows life. He is dark, good looking, dresses well and charming. Anne- Mare describes him as quiet. To Gracie, there is something about him she doesn't like. Her mother blames it on her absent, disappeared father. Gracie sees it as something else. 

He is a business man - but what form that business takes is questionable. In book 2 Seth's true nature is revealed and his presence takes on a more dangerous quality. 

Seth is a prominent figure throughout all the books. As the series unfolds, we learn more about him.
