Thornhelm, of House Morvaine, is the Unseelie King of Summer. Referred to as The Mire King, he is a strong, almost handsome, determined Fae who meets Gracie at the beginning of Book 2 - Strands of Shadow and Truth. 

He finds her fascinating, in particular her red hair. So much so, that he manipulates her into making a Fae Promise, a Faerie bargain. These are never good and are bound in ancient Fae Law. He extracts three kisses from her, three being a magical number, and binds her to him. He wishes to make her his queen.  This promise, haunts Gracie throughout the series. Thornhelm uses underhand tactics, magic and all manner of ruthless actions in attempts to secure Gracie as his prize. 

He is an ancient Fae being, looks timeless, green tinged skin, yet there is something alluring and charming about him to being with. He is empowered with black fire and deadly magic, which he does not hesitate to use. He is able to create portals at will. Portals allow travel to and from various places, they can be conjured out of thin air and seconds later, an individual is somewhere else. 

Thornhelm believes in the ancient magic which flows within the earth itself. He also believes it is his right, to take and keep that which he wants!
