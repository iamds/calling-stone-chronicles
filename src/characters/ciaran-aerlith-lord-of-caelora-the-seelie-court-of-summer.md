He stands beside the wide arched window, half-shadowed, half-lit by the cold Winter sun. Tall. Still. Wings folded behind him — not like Ranolf’s soft white ones, but something golden patterned like a sunset settling over summer fields. 

Golden hair,spills over his shoulders, and his eyes are more gilded than brown. 

Ciaran hails from Caelora. Sent to Winter because the Seelie courts have heard rumours there is a spy. Ciaran observes, watches, learns and becomes more involved than he ever thought possible.
