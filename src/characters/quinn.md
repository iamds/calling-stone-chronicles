Quinn SableWhisker, is a magical black cat with emerald eyes.  He is witty, sarcastic, funny and becomes utterly loyal to Gracie, who to begin with, he just finds amusing. He is an excellent swordsman and in her early time in Limskada, Elyndra, he helps with her learning. He teaches her how to fight. A very useful skill when going on a quest! He enjoys teasing her and spends a lot of his time doing exactly that. He is clever, knowledgable, and a trusted friend. This is what Melinka, a maid in the castle at Limskada, says about him. 



Melinka quickly dips into a curtsy. “Master Sablewhisker,” she murmurs.

Quinn exhales through his nose, looking at her. “At least someone understands proper respect. Greetings Melinka. I hope you’ve missed me.”

She crosses to the dresser and begins to stroke his back. He purrs. 

  “Miss Gracie.” Melinka glances at Quinn. “I have known Master Sablewhisker since I was a child. He has always lived here, watching, listening and knowing…everything.”

Quinn curls his tail, looking very smug.

“His name carries such meaning,” she continues. “Sable for his coat, whisker for his keen senses. But it also signifies wisdom, intelligence, and—” she pauses, lips twitching, “an unshakable opinion of one’s own importance.”

Quinn’s purr lowers toa deep, contented sound. “You say that as if it’s a flaw, Melinka. I prefer to think of it as a well-earned reputation.”
