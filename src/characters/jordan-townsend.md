Jordan is Simon's sidekick. They are good mates. Share a joke and take huge delight in the Hamlet video created during the school dance, where Gracie, finds herself the centre of attention. They are prime examples of the sort of behaviour we DON"T want to see happening in young men. They appear in book 2 Strands of Shadow and Truth and continue to feature throughout the remaining four books. 

Jordan is perhaps, less obvious than Simon. Although both enjoy making her feel uncomfortable and take huge pleasure in making comments, Simon is perhaps the more dangerous. Jordan tends to follow rather than suggest what they could do. He supports Simon.
