Smerdante, King of Unseelie Spring, a High Fae king, is beautiful, manipulative, charming and utterly ruthless. He is extremely dangerous. He craves power and will find it in anyone and anything. 

He is the cause of the Darkness which is destroying Limskada at the start of Book 1 - Threads of Trial and Light. He is a skilled mage. He was once an associate and friend of Temerin. They trained together on the Island of Ystme. But Smerdante, determined to unleash the Untried Passages, turned to dark magic. 

He is pale skinned, with raven - dark hair and violet eyes. His true form is unknown as the glamour he wears never drops. 

We meet him in person at the end of Book 2 - Strands of Shadow and Truth. Until then he is a name, an unseen presence, a dangerous threat.
