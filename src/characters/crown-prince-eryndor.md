In his youth, Prince Eryndor craved adventure and excitement. He left The Seelie Court of Winter, in disguise. He travelled through the Passing Lands to the world of humans. There, he fell in love with a clever, intelligent and beautiful mortal woman. They had a child. A daughter. But the Seelie guards of Winter never stopped looked for the prince and once they had found him, dragged him back to Winter.  He was forbidden ever to leave Winter under pain of death. He left his daughter with a gift. A pendant containing a red stone - The Calling Stone.
