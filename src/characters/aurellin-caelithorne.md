When she was only a child, seven years of age, Aurelin was snatched from the borders of Limskada. Her brother, Prince Dara, searched high and low to find some sign of her, but she had vanished. Her disappearance when Dara was only eleven, turned him into a quiet brooding individual, who hated the Fae and almost anything magical. 

Aurelin could almost be Gracie's twin. Red hair, green eyes, a reckless attitude. and a determination to be more than people think.
