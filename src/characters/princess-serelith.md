Princess Serelith visits The Mortal Court. She comes from Nino, a place found in the far reaches of Limskada, The Mortal realm.  She is a mortal princess we are told. She is stunningly beautiful. Almost the direct opposite of Gracie. Sleek black hair, violet eyes, and a smile which can charm everyone. 

She appears in Book Four, "Weaves of Wisdom and Resolve."

Edmund and Serena are fussing around a figure dressed in a deep violet gown, trimmed in black and silver. Her sheet of silk ebony hair reaches her waist. She smiles up at Tavian. And he… smiles back with the smile he used to keep for me.

My heart twists, sharp and sudden.
