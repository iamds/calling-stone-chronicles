In Elyndra, The Fates or The Moirai recognise destiny as it shown to them. They control an individual's journey of birth, life and death. Clotho, spins a human's thread,  Lachesis weaves it into the strands it must follow and Atropos cuts it at death with a pair of large shears. 

In Gracie's story they recognise that destiny can be changed and where magic is involved anything is possible.
