King Edmund and Queen Serena of Limskada are the rulers of The Mortal Realm. This realm within Elyndra, contains no magic unlike the Fae realms of Spring, Summer, Autumn and Winter. 

The King and Queen have a tragic past, losing their third child, Aurelin, when she was seven. Their land is dying, due to a deadly Darkness, suspected to be the work of Dark Magic under Smerdante, the King of Unseelie Spring. They are desperate to have their land healed and cling to an ancient prophecy regarding a strange visitor. 



A woman steps forward, gliding over the polished floor. She is regal, hiding a sorrow behind her eyes. She touches my cheek with gentle fingers. Her delicate voice is a light summer breeze whispering through trees.

“From distant world and time unknown,

A child shall rise to claim her own.

With Fae-born mage to guide their hand,

And princes twain, united stand.

Through shadowed trials the path is spun,

Till darkness falls and fate is won.”

She holds my gaze. “Gracie. Welcome to Limska.” She studies me.

“Do you wear the Callisvaan, my child?”

“Yes. Your Majesty.”

“May I see it?”

I draw it out. Her fingers graze it and sadness melts like starlight piercing shadows.

“Good,” she breathes. “Now you must rest.”

The two rulers expect loyalty and duty, in everything, which is why, when things go wrong, they refuse to accept anything could relate to them.
