Agorinex is a beautiful stag that finds Gracie when she is alone, feeling rejected and stranded in an abandoned ruin due to the snow.  

Unable to help with maps and charts, Temerin refuses her offer of help and sends her away  in case she distracts Tavian. Gracie is lonely. 

Agorinex appears when she is easy to manipulate. His beauty deceives, his words drip charm, but is he as kind as he seems? Or is he simply trying to break down Gracie's defences.

He appears in Book Two, "Strands of Shadow and Truth"

The air is laced with frost and something else—a scent like melted chocolate edged with cherry and almond. The stag takes a slow step forward, bends one foreleg, and bows.

I curtsey in return. “You are a resplendent creature. Can you understand me?”

He lifts his head, eyes deep sapphire pools. When he speaks, his breath curls in silver vapour between us, seeping into the cold, belonging there. His voice is water sliding over glass. “My Lady Gracielle, I can indeed. Your presence has long been spoken of. But your beauty is greater than any word revealed.”

Heat floods my face. “Thank you…?”

“My name is Agorinex. I am at your service, Lady Gleamweaver.”
