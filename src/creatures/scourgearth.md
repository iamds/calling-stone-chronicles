The Scourgearth is a dragon. Breathing fire and wreaking havoc, it destroys parts of Ebonreach at the end of book 1.  It is intent on finding Gracie. To capture her, snatch her or destroy her is unclear. 

From book 1: 



The night sky isa swirling mass of dark clouds, thick and suffocating, as though the starshide, too afraid to watch. A cold wind howls through the clearing, carryingwith it the scent of earth and something sharper. Blood. Metal.

A shape, red as flame itself, tears through the clouds towards us. Wings the width of a city beat with purpose. Scales, deep vermillion cover its body with veins of molten fire running through its length. Its tail lashes through the nocturnal skies, hurling the stars into chasms of darkness. Claws, razor sharp as forged blades, slash past the moon in a swipe of terror.  The creature’s eyes, if you could even call them eyes, blaze with a fiery light  not of this world. The roar of the monster rips through the night, a sound that splits the very fabric of the earth apart. And when it opens its mouth, the heat sears my skin. 

And when Gracie runs from it, it follows her, intent on destruction.
