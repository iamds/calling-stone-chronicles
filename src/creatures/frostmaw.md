The Frostmaw tunnels beneath the frozen wilderness, moving silently through ancient ice and stone until the moment it strikes. Hunting by vibration and movement, it can sense the faintest tremor through the ground, tracking prey by footsteps and heartbeat alike.

A master of ambush, the Frostmaw erupts from below with explosive force, scattering camps and caravans before its victims can react. Its breath is colder than the deepest winter, capable of freezing flesh, shattering stone, and stealing breath from those caught in its path.

Feared throughout the northern wilds, Frostmaws are said to dwell where the ice is oldest and the cold runs deepest. Few survive an encounter, and tales of their attacks are whispered around campfires across the frozen lands surrounding Skjarthfell.

*"The Frostmaw wants stillness. Don't give it that."* — Ranolf

**Known Habitat**

The deepest ice fields and frozen highlands ofSkjarthfell.

**Signs of Approach** 

• Sudden ground tremors
• Cracks in ice or stone
• Unnatural cold
• Low, grinding rumbles
