The Vraekhal is among the oldest and most feared entities of the northern mountains. It cannot be slain by sword or spell, nor driven away through force. Those who encounter it soon discover that survival depends not upon strength, but upon endurance and honesty.

Where the Vraekhal walks, sound, wind and even magic seem to falter. Storms fall silent. Thoughts become difficult to grasp.The world itself feels diminished beneath its immense and overwhelming presence.

Though it possesses no face or eyes, the Vraekhal perceives every traveller who enters its domain. Fear, love, loyalty, grief and regret are laid bare before it. Secrets become impossible to conceal, and lies collapse beneath its scrutiny. Many who face the Vraekhal describe the experience as a judgement more terrible than death itself.

Ancient stories claim the Vraekhal guards forgotten crossings, hidden paths and places where the boundaries between realms grow thin. Whether it serves as guardian, judge, or something older still remains unknown.

*"This thing... I cannot endure it."* — Ranolf

**Known Habitat**

• Mountain passes and gateways
• Places where the storm stills
• Ancient crossings and thresholds
• Boundaries between realms
• Skjarthfell and its hidden paths

**Signs of Approach** 

• Sudden silence within the storm
• Magic faltering or dying away
• Mist swallowing sound and light
• Thoughts becoming difficult to hold
• A crushing weight upon the spirit

**Survival Notes** 

• Do not resist its presence.
• Speak only what is true.
• Secrets will be torn from you regardless.
• Hold fast to those you trust.
• Stay united, stay still, stay honest.
• There is no escape—only endurance.
