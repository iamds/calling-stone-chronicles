The Noctavere is a monster created by Smerdante. resembling nothing more than a dark, black mist or shadow, it is one of his most powerful creatures. It creates illusions so real, brother turns against brother and King against soldier. It creates and feeds from anger. Even Temerin, with his Seelie magic, cannot find the strength to fight it. 

In Book 2 Gracie and the company encounter the Noctavere, in Ebonreach.
