The Granite Born or The Carven are made from slag. The are creatures of rock. Why they attack the party as they travel though Limskada is not known. We assume the group travelled through their lands. In which case, they might not have been happy about that, not asking permission, etc. 

Swords and traditional weapons cannot hurt them. Using a sword against a rock is it bit useless. 

This is the first sighting. 

"Cracks spider through the cobblestones.Dust and grit pour from the gaps. Then the path buckles—and creatures rise.

Grey-skinned giants, shaped from stone. Their hair falls like eroded cliffs, their eyes smouldering with volcanic red. From beneath the broken surface, they climb.

The horses scream. Guards are thrown. Chaos erupts.

Temerin doesn’t move.

“What are they?” I say.

“The Carven,” he breathes. “Some call them the Graniteborn. Forged from ancient volcanic flow. Nothing natural holds them. They’re shaped by pressure and silence.”

Tavian draws his sword. So does Dara. They move as one toward the nearest creature. 

And still they rise, from the depths of the earth."

Gracie uses both science and magic in her attempt to stop them.
