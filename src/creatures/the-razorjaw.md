The Razorjaw swarm in shoals. They surge through the water and out of it. Their attack comes from the air and their razor sharp teeth are deadly. Found on the river named Wraithwater, They attack the boat and the people. They attack at sunset, swift, deadly. When the light touches Wraithwater's surface they rise. And in their attack, Dara is injured. 

As Wraithwater meets the edge of Ebonreach, it is likely the Razorjaw have Smerdante as a master. As they are bent on destruction, like so many of his creatures, their teeth and speed will be to his advantage.
