The Karthshroud appears as little more than drifting mist, often mistaken for harmless fog rolling across mountain passes and frozen valleys. Yet beneath its shifting vapours lies an ancient intelligence that preys upon doubt, exhaustion and hope.

Rather than hunt through violence, the Karthshroud deceives. It creates false paths, hidden clearings and visions of safety where none exist. Victims may glimpse sunlight through the fog, hear distant voices, or feel the comforting illusion of warmth in the bitter cold. Drawn onward by these promises, they slowly lose sight of reality.

The creature delights in separation and isolation.Its thickening mists can cut travellers off from companions, campfires and familiar landmarks, leaving them alone with the whispers that guide them ever forward. Those who follow too far are often led toward cliffs, crevasses and deadly drops concealed until the final moment.

Stories claim that those taken by the Karthshroud are never seen again. Whether it consumes them, claims them, or simply leads them beyond recovery, none can say. The Karthshroud keeps its secrets well.

*"It draws you in. Makes the way ahead seem easier. Safer. And then it leads you where the ground disappears."* — Casabian

**Known Habitat**

• High mountain passes
• Cliff edges and ravines
• Cold valleys and gorges
• Regions where wind meets rising mist

**Signs of Approach** 

• Mist rolling in without rain
• Light dulling suddenly
• Wind shifting unsettlingly
• Whispers carried on the air
• Paths that feel too easy

**Survival Notes** 

• Stay close to companions and firelight.
• Do not follow clearings or bright openings in the mist.
• Ignore whispered promises or sensations of warmth.
• If separated, stop and listen for your companions.
• Magic light can weaken, but not fully dispel, the Karthshroud.
