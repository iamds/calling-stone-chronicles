The Dreknhound guards the entrance to The Silent Lands. Similar to Cerberus of Greek Mythology, he resembles some kind of 'dog' but has two heads, three barbed tails and teeth which can rip anything and anyone in two. 

From Book Four, 'Weaves of Wisdom and Resolve.'

The path ends at the entrance to a cavern shaped like a jagged stone jaw. Something lies across it.

*A dog?*
*Well… something that began as a dog and then kept going.*

Its pelt is wet-ash grey, thick enough to hide whole forests of thorns beneath. Two massive heads rest upon paws the size of shields. Three long, barbed tails curl and uncurl lazily. Each exhale fogs the ground with dark vapour.
