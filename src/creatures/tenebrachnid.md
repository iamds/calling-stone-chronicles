The Tenebrachnid is a  gigantic spider born of shadow and darkness. It lurks out of sight.Native to Ebonreach, it is a creature of Smerdante. It has many eyes and able to spin a thread so fine, it is almost beautiful in its ability to ensare. 

When the company meet it in Ebonreach they have been warned by Gracie the way is dangerous. She is ignored. The creature traps the remainder of the group and chooses to speak to Gracie alone. 

It gives her some disturbing news. 

From Book 2
