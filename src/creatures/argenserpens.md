The Argenserpens lives in The Silver Lands, known as Argenta. A vast desert of silver sand. It will only allow passage safely through its wastes, if a human, or mortal being, answers a riddle. 

The guards, princes and Gracie are all confused and worried by the riddle it poses. It appears to make no sense. But perhaps the answer lies in Gracie's past and a memory of her father.
