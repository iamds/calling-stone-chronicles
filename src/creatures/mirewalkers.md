The Mirewalkers are men - like beasts formed from rot, earth and mire.  Trolls from legend. They are the soldiers, servants of Thornhelm, King of Unseelie Summer, The Mire King, a High Fae King. They follow Thornhelm without question, fight for him, hold prisoners for him, obey his wishes without question. They are brutish, ugly, monsters.  We first meet them early in Book 2, when Thornhelm meets Gracie for the first time. They have Prince Tavian as their prisoner. 

From Book 2 'Strands of Shadow and Truth"

"The shapes don’t fade. Instead, they grow—massive lumps rising from the earth, dragging themselves up as if the land is surrendering itself to some hidden force.
