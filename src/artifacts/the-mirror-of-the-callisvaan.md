The Mirror of the Callisvaan is stored in Smerdante's castle in Ebonreach. 

Gracie and Tavian find the stolen Callisvaan in Book 3 - Ribbons of Destiny and Belief

"The room is empty, save for a tall, framed mirror at the far end of the room. I hurry to it. Tavian is close behind. I stare. A single crack runs from the top to its bottom. Around the frame are markings, patterns and runes etched into the wood. 

“What does it say Gracie? Can you read it?Is it there?”

“Sh, Tavian. I … I have to think.”

He paces back and forth, boots tapping on the marble floor.

I let my fingers trail over the markings, feeling the pattern, recognising some of the scratches. 

*Come on Gracie. You don’t have the luxury of time.* The pendant glows, beginning to pulse. 

The markings blur and swim at first — jagged strokes etched into the frame. They don’t sit still like words in a book are supposed to do. They behave like when I stare at a page. They coil, knot, and weave across the cracks. My eyes snag on curves that mirror each other, spirals,circles, shapes, repeating in rhythm. Not sentences. *Patterns.*

When I trace them with my finger, colours pulse faintly — silver, violet, green. The fragments join, and in my head they don’t sound like normal words might. They fall like a poem.

They come to life, and I read. 

“Three shards will call and lights shall bind,

The hand that weaves love’s truth must guide.

Claim the last — love’s kiss to taste,

Or Shears of Fate the knot shall waste.”

I look into the mirror, see Tavian behind me. The crack splinters. Behind a veil, the third Callisvaan rests on a golden stand.

“Tavian, together, we must take it together, and kiss me.”

“Gracie, is this the right time to …”

“Just kiss me, as we reach in. Do it.”

His mouth claims mine as our hands touch the stand. My fingers curl around the Callisvaan drawing it out. The mirror cracks wide, fragments raining down like jagged stars.

             Shadows stream from every wall, and something like a banshee wails a warning to its master. *Smerdante.* Tavian hauls mea way. *I did it. We did it. The third Callisvaan. The prophecy fulfilled.*
