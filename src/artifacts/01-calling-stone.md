The Calling Stone appears to ordinary people as a simple pendant with a red stone. But it is The Callisvaan, one of three magical identical pendants which contain powerful magic.

Together the three Callisvaan have the ability to heal Limskada and restore the mortal realm to its former state. 

Two of the Callisvaan are held by Gracie and Temerin, the mage, one of The Grey Brethren. Gracie was given hers by her father before he left. it was the last thing he gave her. The missing third Callisvaan, is held by Smerdante. Locked away and hidden, it is the reason for the quest to Ebonreach and Smerdante's castle, to find and secure the missing Callisvaan.
