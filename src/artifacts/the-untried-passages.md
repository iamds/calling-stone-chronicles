The Untried Passages are a companion to The Book of Marble. Together they produce the balance of Light and Dark needed to keep the Seelie and Unseelie magic in equilibrium .

However, where the spells and guidance of The Book of Marble, assist and support the training of the Grey Brethren, there are warnings associated with The Untried Passages. Casting their spells and enchantments is  dangerous. When Smerdante, during his training on Ystme, experiments with them, he releases a shadow power which begins his descent into power associated with evil.
