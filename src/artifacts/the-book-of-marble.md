The Book of Marble is a book associated with the positive Light magic of the Seelie Fae. it is a book of spells and information. Housed on Ystme, the island that trains wizards and mages, it is held in high esteem and treated with reverence. 

Its name is derived from its white pages which glow like marble.

It's power is not to be ignored or taken lightly. 

Temerin speaks of it with huge respect.
