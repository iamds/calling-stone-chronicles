These three artefacts, given in trust by The Recluse, have individual properties. 

Inkthirst does not read ,it remembers what has been written in code. 

Oathglass warms when danger listens.

Whispercatcher catches what is spoken.

Together they can reveal and hold the truth. 

Three ways of knowing.
