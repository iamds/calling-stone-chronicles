In the Seelie Court of Spring, Florindral, House Florindral, fresh starts, new beginnings and renewal of life thrive. Magic is at its strongest at dawn or in a moment of new growth. It is home to poets, idealists, and those who seek rebirth.  Veradessa is capital of Veranthia. 

Veradessa — Capital of the SeelieSpring Realm (Veranthia)

A city of living bloom and ancient magic.

Veradessa rises like a garden carved into the sky — spiralling terraces layered with flowering vines, glass-petal spires that shimmer in soft sunrise hues, and bridges grown from living branches that knot and unfurl with every season. The air is warm, honey-bright, fragrant with blossoms that never wilt.

Rivers of silver-green water thread through its streets, feeding luminous pools where the Spring mages weave healing spells. Music drifts everywhere — soft wind chimes, birdsong, and the hum of enchantment woven into growing things.

But beneath the beauty lies old power: roots that remember ancient bargains, blossoms that listen, and a throne carved from a single living tree whose heartwood pulses with the rhythm of the realm itself.

Veradessa is gentleness, growth, and rebirth—
but also the reminder that Spring can coax life… or choke it.
