IN the Seelie court of Spring, Florindral, House Florindral, fresh starts, new beginnings and renewal of life thrive. Magic is at its strongest at dawn or in a moment of new growth. It is home to poets, idealists, and those who seek rebirth.  Veradessea is capital of Veranthia. 

Veradessa — Capital of the SeelieSpring Realm (Veranthia)

A city of living bloom and ancient magic.

Veradessa rises like a garden carved into the sky — spiralling terraces layeredwith flowering vines, glass-petal spires that shimmer in soft sunrise hues, andbridges grown from living branches that knot and unfurl with every season. Theair is warm, honey-bright, fragrant with blossoms that never wilt.

Rivers of silver-green water thread through its streets, feedingluminous pools where the Spring mages weave healing spells. Music driftseverywhere — soft wind chimes, birdsong, and the hum of enchantment woven intogrowing things.

But beneath the beauty lies old power: roots that remember ancientbargains, blossoms that listen, and a throne carved from a single living treewhose heartwood pulses with the rhythm of the realm itself.

Veradessa is gentleness, growth, and rebirth—
but also the reminder that Spring can coax life… or choke it.
