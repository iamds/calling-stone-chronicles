The Seelie Winter Court. House Seranthiel. 

Realm: The Seranstrae Dominion – Realm of Honour and Starlight. The Winter Court.

The Seelie Winter Court is lodged in the capital of Seranstrae, Serandor. King Tharos and Queen Maeriel rule over this court. Governed by wisdom, belief in magic of the light, they are guardians of law and heritage. They are the most powerful of the Seelie Courts. 

Serandor is a wondrous place.
