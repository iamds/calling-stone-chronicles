The Silent Lands are ruled over by Queen Noxira. She likes to play games, with minds, and people. 

The Silent Lands are vast, sprawling under the Seelie Winter lands, almost forgotten. To reach them you enter at The Gateway Beneath the World. Following the route downwards you have to pass over The Shattered Crossing , The Swamps of Utterance, where whispering voices threaten to devour your mind, and then face the guardian to The Silent Lands, The Dreknhound. 

There is a vast library in this place. It is here ,Temerin hopes they will discover the information regarding the elixir he is desperate to find. 

From Book Four, "Weaves of Wisdom and Resolve."

The library is huge. A high-domed ceiling dominates walls lined with books, scrolls and thickly bound papers. From it hang a series of chandeliers, flames flickering with a strange silvery light. A heavy grey carpet, patterned with runes, signs and shapes, covers the stone floor. Temerin sits at a huge desk made from swollen vines and heavy tree trunks. In front of him are several thick volumes. The others each have their heads in various books. 

The Silent Lands are a dark, dangerous place. Passages with a mind of their own. Halls which show only despair and pathways filled with traps. 

From Book Four, 

Stone presses close on either side, slick and cold. A faint green glow pulses through cracks in the walls — not enough to see by, only enough to feel watched. The air thickens with damp earth and rot.

Then, slowly, the tunnel widens.
