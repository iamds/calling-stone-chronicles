The Seelie Court of Winter, House Seranthiel  - is the most powerful of all the Seelie Courts.  The Seranstrae Dominium is a realm of starlight and snow. Frost, Ice and at the heart of it lies Serandor — Capital of the Seelie Winter Court

Serandor stands like a fortress of star-forged ice upon the crest of the Frostspire Plateau. It is a city of soaring geometry: sharp angles softened by shimmering frost, domes etched with constellations, and bridges that glitter like frozen moonlight.

The streets are paved with skyglass, a translucent mineral that records light and patterns beneath its surface — every footstep briefly echoes with starlight. Above the city rise the Seranstrine Towers, slender white spires shaped like inverted icicles, each crowned with a lantern of captured winter flame.

Soft auroras drift constantly across the sky, colouring the air with pale ribbons of blue, violet, and white. They respond to emotion, magic, and ceremony — a subtle heartbeat of the Winter Court itself.

Serandor is known for:

•	Star-magic, woven into law, protection, and ancient oaths.

•	Quiet strength and honour, the defining traits of Winter.

•	The Hall of Echoes, where ancestors’ voices whisper advice to Winter’s rulers.

•	The Sable Guard, elite warriors who wield blades infused with frostlight.

Though serene, Serandor carries weight:

a reminder that Winter is not coldness, but clarity; not death, but the stillness from which rebirth begins.

The Winter Court is also home, in its furthest reaches, to the Nixpardus.
