Limskada is the Mortal Realm of Elyndra. It is ruled over by King Edmund and Queen Serena. Limskada is a land under attack. Its once beautiful landscape is marred and broken. Scarred by the darkness sent by Smerdante, hoping to force them to part with the Callisvaan secured by Temerin. 

It is under the protection of the Seelie Courts, Winter, Spring, Summer, Autumn, but even their magic seems powerless in stopping the darkness as it tries to overtake the land. 

It is from the castle of Limskada, that the Quest for the Callisvaan begins. Here Gracie meets the royal family, including Prince Dara and Prince Tavian.

Limskada is home to woodlands, forests and a desert.
