Caelora is a place of sunlight. Its strength drawn from heat. Ruled by King Auren and Queen Selindra. The heir to the throne is Prince Caelis, soon to be King as his father wishes to pass the burden of the crown to his son. Its people are courageous, full of vitality, they may display war like strength. Everything blazes in Caelora from its people to its court.  It is a proud, charismatic, glorious land. Its inhabitants fly. Refusing to surrender their wings in an ancient war, the Fae who live there glide and swoop on the air currents that surround the region. 

Solcaris is the capital of Caelora. It rises like a golden mirage about the Sunfields, a city build from sandstone that glows as if lit from within. Every surface carries a warm shimmer, touched by the eternal high-summer light that blesses the realm. 

The city spirals outward from the Solar Spire, a tall tower of molten-glass architecture that captures sunlight and scatters it across the realm in radiant arcs. Sunbirds nest in its balconies- small luminous creatures whose feathers shed sparks of harmless fire. 

Broad causeways of pale limestone curve between pools of turquoise water, surrounded by terraces of bright flowers that lean toward passers-by as though greeting them. Windchimes made of polished shells and sun-forged metal hang from every archway, humming with the realm's energy. 

Solcaris is known for: 

Heat woven magic, channelled through golden threads bound to the realm's light. 

Celebrations, where music and dance last from sunrise to starlight. 

Sunwrights, artisans who sculpt living light into glass, armour, jewellery and weapons.

A culture built on joy, courage, generosity, and the fierce belief that light must be defended. 

But beneath its brilliant, Solcaris carries a warning glow– for Summer's passion is mighty and its storms when roused can burn as quickly as they brighten.
