Ebonreach is Smerdante's realm. The Unseelie Court of Spring. House Nyxareth. A realm of Secrets and Shadows.  Full of beauty and hidden deception. Its beauty is only an illusiong for underneath its veneer, shadows peel from walls, whispers float like smoke and those who inhabit Ebonreach mask themselves in its deception. It shows itself to be elegant, attractive and alluring, the truth reveals monsters, false glamour and lies. Smerdante use magic to hide what he does not want seen.  He controls through manipulation, fear and suggestion. He does not play fair and shadows do his bidding. When Gracie finds herself there, she is in terrible danger.
