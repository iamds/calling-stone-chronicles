Argenta, also known as The Silver Lands, is a vast desert of silver sand. It shimmers like glitter. Its ash is deadly. When crossing the vast plain, faces have to be covered against the wind and heat. Strangely, it does not affect the animals, horses, but can kill humans. 

Its guardian is the Argenserpens. A creature of ancient lore. A creature that demands the answer to a riddle to ensure safe passage across the sands. 

From Book One, "Threads of Trial and Light." 

I wanderto the edge of camp, staring out. The desert glimmers like lace spun fromglass—beautiful, and lethal. I sink my gloved hand into the sand. It’s soft assifted powder. When I pull away, silver dust slips through my fingers in acascade of light.

A desert made of stardust, waiting to kill.
