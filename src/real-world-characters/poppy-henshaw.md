Poppy Henshaw is the primary teacher Gracie works with in Marshview Primary School. She has a regular placement in the school and does a lot of Art with the primary children. Poppy is open, friendly, has a huge smile and loves her job. She allows Gracie to teach Art to the students and do reading work, among all the other things that primary teachers have to do.
