Amy shared her lunch with Gracie when they were at nursery. They have been best friends ever since. Amy stands with and for Gracie through thick ad thin. They share confidences, secrets, and everything else. Amy is bright, she doesn't struggle like Gracie, but she insists that Gracie is better than she thinks. 

Amy also takes art but her style uses pale colours, pastels and gentle shades. She admires everything Gracie does in Art. Over Christmas, when Anne-Marie and Seth go away for a couple of weeks, Gracie stays with Amy. It is Amy who sorts out the problems between Tommy and Gracie. She goes to the school dance as Titania. In Books 1 onwards she has a boyfriend, who hopes to go to Drama School, he is in the Sixth form, Piers.
