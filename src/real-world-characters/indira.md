Indira is a very close friend of Gracie's. She is very bright and hopes to do medicine at University after her A levels. She wants to be a doctor. 

She is family orientated and always helps out with relations if she can.  She always submits assignments on time, is rarely, if ever, late. 

She is also protective of Gracie. When Gracie has a really hard time in Book 1, Indira like Amy is 'there' never wavering or backing down. 

She is good in a crisis. Coming from a large family she opens her arms to anyone who needs her. 

At the school dance, she wears her grandmother's sari, looks stunning and meets a girl dressed as Robin Hood - Rhea. They remain together until the end of book 3.
