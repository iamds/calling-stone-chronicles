Laurel Whitmore is a year eleven who, like Gracie, is a target for unwanted male attention.  She takes as much as she can until it becomes too much. She lashes out and is punished for it. 

With current systems failing to recognise misogyny when it both hidden and disguised, Laurel is a prime example of what can happen when things are not handled correctly.
