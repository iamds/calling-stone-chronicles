The Art Teacher, Sally Raynesworth, is a constant support for Gracie. She recognises Gracie's talent in Art and encourages her to find her own style and approach to the tasks she undertakes for GCSE. Gracie uses her classroom as an escape from the school bully and loves everything to do with Art. Her teacher allows her to experiment, shows her ways of doing things and guides her with exposure to different artists. She lends her books, suggests websites and recognises Gracie's inherent talent even when Gracie falters. 

She becomes a strong support and good friend. Sally is the person Gracie turns to when things get tough. She values her support more and more as the books continue. 

In 'Strands of Shadow and Truth', it is Miss Raynesworth who listens to her concerns over the boys, the video and everything that happens.
