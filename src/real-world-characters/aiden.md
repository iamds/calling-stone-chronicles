Aiden is a close friend of Theo. He is an artist who shapes metal and creates sculptures. He is a gentle personality who understands the world of Art and recognises what it is capable of. He doesn't force or pretend at anything. He is a genuine person who gives Theo what he is looking for. 

A lovely caring, messy indiviudal.
