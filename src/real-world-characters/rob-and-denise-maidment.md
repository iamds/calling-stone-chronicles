Rob and Denise Maidment are caring parents who support Tommy in everything he does. In Book 3 Denise becomes Gracie's English tutor. She sees Gracie's problems and anxiety first hand. 

When Tommy's behaviour causes Seth to insist he speaks to them, they support their son, giving logical and sensible reasons for the incident and Tommy's behaviour. 

Gracie first meets them at the hospital when Tommy has been injured. Although they are extremely worried about their son, they give Tommy and Gracie time together. They are the sort of parents most teenagers would appreciate having, as they set sensible rules, acknowledge life is tough at sixteen and are reasonable.
