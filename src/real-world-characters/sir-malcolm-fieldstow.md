Sir Malcolm Fieldstow is a local patron of the arts. He owns the Greengold Arts Institute a local college which encourages excellence in the arts. He is a prominent figure in anything which involves the arts and is a close friend of Theo. He supports the rebuild of The Whispering Canvas after the arson attack.
