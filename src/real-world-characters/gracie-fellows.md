Gracie Fellows is the sixteen year old central character of The Calling Stone Chronicles. Starting with Threads of Trial and Light, we follow her journey in the Fantasy realms of the Fae alongside her battles with year eleven and GCSE. A talented artist, but struggling student, Gracie also as a fractured home life. Her mother, Anne - Marie, as successful lawyer, has little time for her daughter's problems or indeed for her daughter herself. Her attention is on her boyfriend Seth Ravenwood. 
Gracie has three close friends who do their best to support her. Amy, clever, supportive best friend from nursery. Indira, intelligent, driven wanting to be a doctor. And Tommy Maidment, another friend, perhaps her most supportive. Protective, caring and aspiring footballer, Tommy sticks with Gracie through all the bullying until he doesn't!

In the Fantasy realm of Limskada, Gracie travels on a quest to find the third Callisvaan, the stone that will heal the dying realm. With her, Dara, brooding, serious heir to the throne, who hates her from the beginning. And Tavian, silver haired, charming, flirtatious. A storyteller, a friend. Someone she has to save.
