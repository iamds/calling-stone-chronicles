Oliver Hart is about as different to Seth as it's possible to be. Honest, truthful, genuine and caring. He forms a relationship with Anne-Marie after all the problems with Seth. 

He is a soft-wear designer. Steady, thoughtful and intelligent. There is a warmth to him. Something about him puts people at ease. 

They meet at a special function. Anne- Marie is hesitate and worried for Gracie, so for these reasons she takes things very slow and carefully with Oliver. As things have changed between Gracie and her mother by the time book four occurs, Gracie is accepting and happy for her mother, even though the only thing Oliver can cook, is pasta!
