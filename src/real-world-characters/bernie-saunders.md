Bernie Saunders is a close friend of Sally Raynesworth. She is the key lecturer for the Advanced Diploma. UAL Level 3 Extended Diploma in Creative Practice: Art, Design & Communication’ 

She is creative, energetic and full of life. She brings out the best in her students and expects more. Although demanding she has a way of recognising talent and encouraging it.
