Maisie appears in book 3, Ribbons of Destiny and Belief. When Gracie takes matters of misogyny into her own hands, Maisie is impressed. 

She thinks a group should be formed for girls. There, they could speak about their concerns, and support each other. She manages to persuade Gracie to start it up. Although Gracie is reluctant, she realises it has value and would probably help the girls in her school.
