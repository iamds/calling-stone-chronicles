Where Gracie is red haired, wears glasses, MeMe is blonde with glasses. They bond instantly and with Rowan form a tight friendship. 

MeMe hopes to be a designer. She sews and makes stunning, original, eye-catching designs. She is married although we don't hear much about her husband, not do we meet him. But we get the impression he is supportive of her. 

When it's needed, MeMe is a shoulder to cry on.  She is solid, dependable and talented.
