Kellan Morgrave is a psychiatrist. He visits Gracie at home in connection with the court case. Appearing for the first time in Book 4 - Weaves of Wisdom and Resolve - he seems to have a knack at appearing in places Gracie least expects him.  He remembers everything and is a good listener. Always the professional, he is calm, sees what others may miss and highly respected in his field. He gives the appearance of being respectable and trustworthy, but is there something we don't know?
