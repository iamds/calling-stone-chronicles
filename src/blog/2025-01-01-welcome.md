Welcome to the official home of **The Calling Stone Chronicles**.

This is where you'll find everything about the series — the books, the realms, the characters, the creatures and the stories behind them all.

The five book series follows the challenges and successes of Gracie Fellows a sixteen, in Threads of Trial and Light, dyslexic teenager who finds herself thrown into a magical realm of Light and Dark Magic. 

Elyndra, the Fae Realm houses nine courts, four Seelie and four Unseelie. The ninth court is that of the mortal realm, LImskada, where she meets Crown Prince Dara and Prince Tavian.

On the quest for the stolen Callisvaan, she encounters monsters, prejudice and learns things about herself she couldn't possibly have known. 

In the real world, she has the challenge of bullying to deal with, together with her struggles of school work and the ever-present threat of her upcoming exams. 

At home, Seth Ravenwood, her mother's boyfriend, is a monster of a different type. Charming, pleasant and utterly predatory.

Over the five books, Gracie learns what is means to love, to experience sorrow and loss, but ultimately to be herself and to survive.
